# Terrova Brand Identity (CI)

## Core Philosophy
Terrova’s identity is built around “verifiable evidence for crop-risk decisions.” The design sits between climate stress, insurance discipline, and protocol reliability.

## Visual System: White/Green Mono
We use a high-contrast, technical "White/Green" system that emphasizes clarity and cryptographic certainty.

### Color Palette
- **Background:** `#FFFFFF` (Pure White) - Suggests clarity and transparency.
- **Primary Accent:** `#10B981` (Emerald Green) - Used for verification states, active nodes, and primary CTAs.
- **Text Primary:** `#0F172A` (Slate Dark) - Ensuring maximum legibility.
- **Secondary Surfaces:** `#F1F5F9` (Light Slate) - Used for cards and secondary backgrounds.
- **Borders:** `#E2E8F0` - Subtle, grid-like division of space.

### Typography
- **Primary Font:** `JetBrains Mono` (Monospace)
- **Usage:** Used for headlines, body copy, and UI elements to reinforce the "infrastructure-as-code" and "verifiable data" nature of the protocol.

### Design Principles
1. **Grid-Based Layout:** Use grid overlays and thin-line borders to suggest geospatial precision.
2. **Monochrome Imagery:** Desaturated or monochromatic satellite textures with green active-state highlights.
3. **Evidence-First:** Visuals should look like data (hashes, coordinates, timestamps) rather than decorative graphics.
4. **Modest Radii:** Border radius should be consistent (approx. `0.5rem` or `8px`) to maintain a professional, slightly sharp edge.

## UI Behavior
- **Primary Buttons:** Solid emerald green with white/dark text.
- **Badges:** 
  - `Verified`: Green outline/tint.
  - `Pending`: Neutral slate tint.
  - `Risk Signal`: Red/Warm tint.
- **Animations:** Subtle, magnetic scroll effects and sliding transitions to make the data feel "live" and responsive.
