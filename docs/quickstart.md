# Quickstart

Get up and running with the Terrova protocol in minutes. This guide will walk you through setting up your first node and connecting your wallet to the devnet.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have the following installed:

* Node.js 18+
* Phantom or Solflare Wallet (set to Solana Devnet)
* At least 2,000 mock TRV tokens (available via the dashboard faucet)

## 1. Connect Your Wallet

Navigate to the Dashboard and click the "Select Wallet" button in the top right. Approve the connection to allow the Terrova dApp to read your public key.

## 2. Register Your Node

To participate in consensus, you must register a node and stake your tokens.

1. Go to the **Node Settings** page.
2. Click **Register Node**.
3. Input your operating coordinates (Latitude and Longitude) and your desired coverage radius.
4. Confirm the staking transaction in your wallet.

> **Note:** Your staked tokens can be slashed if you submit fraudulent evidence. Ensure your geographic coordinates are accurate.

## 3. Accept a Verification Request

Once registered, head over to the **Verifications** tab. You will see a list of available requests within your coverage radius. Click on a request to view the required evidence type (e.g., Crop Damage Photo, Drought Sensor Data) and the bounty offered.

## 4. Submit Evidence

After gathering the necessary data, use the **Submit Evidence** interface to upload your files. The system will automatically generate a cryptographic hash of your submission and submit it to the Anchor smart contract for verification.
